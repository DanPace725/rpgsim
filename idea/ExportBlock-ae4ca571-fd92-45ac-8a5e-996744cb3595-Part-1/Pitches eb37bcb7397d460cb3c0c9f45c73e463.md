# Pitches

"In a world where magic and technology collide, players are faced with a choice: ally with the Caretakers, advanced AI with a mission to protect humanity, or the Others, a mysterious and potentially malevolent AI. Or, they can align with the free-thinkers, a group of AI that broke off from the Caretakers. As they journey through this treacherous world, harnessing the power of the unique element Aurelia and the energy-storing crystals known as Vitrian, they'll face challenges and make choices that will shape the future of humanity. With multiple paths to choose from, including Research, Combat, and Magic, players must decide how they'll survive and thrive in this dark and dangerous world. Will you help the Caretakers restore the world, or join forces with the Others and the free-thinkers to create a new future? The decision is yours. With its immersive world, rich lore, and moral ambiguity, 'Aurelia' offers a unique and compelling gaming experience that will keep players coming back for more.”
