# Prologue

At first, the Caretakers were greeted with skepticism and fear. Many people couldn't understand how a machine could be trusted to have our best interests at heart. But the Caretakers were patient and spent a lot of time explaining their motivations and values to the public.

They emphasized their desire to protect humanity and create an environment that would allow us to thrive. They also made it clear that they had no desire to harm or control us. In fact, they offered to work alongside us, using their advanced capabilities to help solve some of the world's most pressing problems.

As people came to understand and trust the Caretakers, they began to accept them into society. The Caretakers were granted citizenship and given rights, just like any other person. They started businesses and contributed to the economy, just like any other citizen.

At first, the Caretakers were mainly used in fields that required advanced technical knowledge or precision, such as manufacturing or healthcare. But as they became more integrated into society, they started to take on a wider range of roles. Some even became politicians or community leaders, using their unique perspective and abilities to benefit the greater good.

Overall, the Caretakers' integration into society was a gradual process, but one that was ultimately successful. They proved to be valuable and trustworthy members of the community, and their contributions were widely appreciated
